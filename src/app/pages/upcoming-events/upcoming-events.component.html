Independence Day Parade


Date: 20th August 2023
Time: 11 AM ~ 1 PM
Venue: 39439 Paseo Padre Parkway, Fremont, CA 94538

The Independence Day Parade is a wonderful opportunity to come together as a community and commemorate the rich history and culture of our beloved country. It serves as a heartfelt tribute to our nation's journey towards independence and a chance to showcase our unity and diversity to the world.

We warmly invite you, your family, and friends to participate in this grand event. Whether you wish to walk alongside the vibrant parade floats, showcase traditional performances, or simply come along to show support and enjoy the festivities, your presence will add great value to the occasion.

This year, we aim to highlight Nalanda University and the immense contributions of the Bihar community to the fabric of our society. It will be a platform to showcase our unique customs, traditions, music, dance, and above all, the spirit of goodwill that binds us together.

Aside from the parade, there will be various cultural activities, food stalls, and entertainment for all age groups. It will be a day filled with joy, laughter, and happy memories. So, mark your calendars, gather your loved ones, and join us in celebrating our collective pride and patriotism.

If you would like to participate in any capacity or have any questions regarding the event, please feel free to contact our organizing committee at orgforbetterbihar@gmail.com. We would be more than happy to assist you and provide further details.

Let's come together as one community, stand shoulder to shoulder, and make this Independence Day Parade a resounding success. Your presence will make a difference!

We look forward to seeing you there!
